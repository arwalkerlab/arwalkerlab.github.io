Spring ACS - Atlanta, GA

2026-03-06 [Updated: 2026-03-06] :: ARW

The Walker Lab attended Spring ACS 2026 in Atlanta, GA. Victoria and Solomon both presented posters in the COMP poster session, and Solomon had an oral presentation in the Medicinal Chemistry session. Alice presented the lab’s work in an oral presentation for the Presidential Event: Human, Computer and Artificial Intelligence symposium, and attended a celebratory dinner for Dr. Weitao Yang with her PhD adviser Andres Cisneros.
